Thrift Things 2025 Sales & Pricing Analysis
Tools: MS Excel, MySQL, Tableau
Category: Retail Analytics / Business Data Analysis

Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end retail analytics workflow analyzing 2025 inventory data from Thrift Things. The goal was to uncover sales trends, top-performing brands, category performance, and negotiation patterns to provide actionable business insights.

Key Features & Analysis
Data Cleaning & Preparation:
1. Imported raw CSV into MySQL, handled unsold items and null values to ensure accurate analysis.
2. Created calculated fields and pivot tables in Excel to explore trends and patterns.

SQL Analysis:
1. Identified top sales months, top brands by total sales value, average sold price per category, and most sold product categories.
2. Calculated negotiation variance and percentage of posted price captured for each product category.

Visualization:
1. Built an interactive Tableau dashboard showing sales volume, pricing patterns, and negotiation efficiency.
2. Dual-axis charts highlight categories with highest price gaps and lowest captured percentages.

Insights:
1. Seasonal trends and top-selling months identified for inventory planning.
2. Top brands and categories by total sales value were highlighted for marketing focus.
3. Negotiation variance analysis revealed which categories are most discounted, informing pricing strategy.
